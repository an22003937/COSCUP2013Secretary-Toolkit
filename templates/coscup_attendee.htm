報到資訊與會場規則
  1. 大會報到台設於台北國際中心一樓大廳（近莊敬路），於 08:15 開始受理報到，請遵循會場工作人員指示報到。報到時將領取識別證掛繩，「識別證＋識別證掛繩」兩者缺一均不得進入會議廳內。
  2. 歡迎搭乘大眾運輸工具與會，請參考 http://coscup.org/2013/zh-tw/venue/ 。若您開車前來，請參考信義區停車資訊導引系統 http://go.coscup.org/coscup13-parking-info
  3. 今年大會不備中餐，歡迎與夥伴一同前往會場附近餐廳用餐。
  4. 會議廳內禁止飲食，並請將所有設備設為靜音或振動。
  5. 嚴禁使用會場網路進行網路攻擊
出門前的準備，及攜帶物品
  1. 如遇颱風或其他特殊情形，請密切注意 COSCUP Blog、Facebook、Plurk、Twitter 或 Google+，我們將會視情況公布是否停辦或順延。
  2. {% if foreigner=='1' %}{% else %}識別證一定要帶，也{% endif %}別忘了帶名片來結識新朋友。（如果您是學生，可考慮自行印製一些名片，方便交換聯絡方式。）
  3. 3G 網卡：大會備有 WiFi，但無法保證品質，歡迎攜帶 3G 網卡 。又，大會禁用個人熱點，請以接線方式使用 3G 網路。
  4. 行動電源：會議室內無電源，會議室外充電區電源有限，如有需要請自備行動電源。
  5. 網路線：會議室外充電區另備有有線網路座，歡迎有需要的朋友自備網路線使用。
  6. 環保杯：現場紙杯有限，歡迎自備飲水容器
  7. 出門前的準備請確保筆電及其他設備中各項軟體都已升級，請勿利用會場網路大量下載檔案

COSCUP 2013 Reminder for Attendee
coscup.org

Dear friend {{nickname}}

We would like to remind you that COSCUP 2013 is coming in a week!

  • Date: Aug 3rd and 4th, 2013 (Saturday and Sunday).
  • Location: Taipei International Convention Center (TICC), please visit http://coscup.org/2013/en/venue/ for details.
The first things you should know
  1. In case of bad weather or any unexpected conditions, the COSCUP team would take adequate actions to respond.(e.g. postpone or cancel the meeting, etc.) Please keep updating our latest announcements on COSCUP Blog, Facebook, Plurk, Twitter or Google+ .
  2. The registration starts at 8:15am.
    3. {% if foreigner=='1' %}
  3. Please collect your badge from our registration counter on the left hand side of Main (East) entrance of TICC. 
    4. {% else %}
  4. We’ve sent you the pass / badge last week, please bring your badge for registration. If you haven’t got one, please contact attendee@coscup.org with your name and email address.
    5. {% endif %}
  5. You may come to TICC by public transportation, please check http://coscup.org/2013/en/venue/ for reference.
  6. If you drive, please check http://go.coscup.org/coscup13-parking-info for parking assistance. (The website is Chinese only)
  7. Lunch will NOT be served by COSCUP team this year, please enjoy your lunch with friends at nearby restaurants.
  8. No food & drinks in conference room, also, please set your equipments to silent or vibrate mode only.
  9. DO NOT commence cyber attack in the meeting.
  10. Please upgrade all necessary software in advance, downloading activity in the meeting may affect network quality.
Please consider to bring the following gadgets with you

3G network card

There will be WiFi in the conference, but we can not guarantee the quality or bandwidth of the network. Please consider to bring your own 3G network card and use USB tethering to surf the net. WiFi Hotspot is prohibited.

Power banks, batteries

No power plugs available in conference room & limited power supply area around the corridor. Please bring your own power solutions to have a better experience.

Ethernet cable

Wired connection will be provided at power supply area, be prepared when you need it.

Name cards

It's good to keep in touch with people you have met.

Reusable cup with cap

It’s the best way to assure your water supply.
